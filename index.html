About Me

I love teaching! During undergrad I majored in pyschology and planned to go into guidance counseling, but after my first teaching internship in the summer of 2011 I knew teaching was for me. I love young students' energy and eagerness to learn, and particularly enjoy working one-on-one with individual families in a family engagement role.

After graduating Union College I taught 4th grade for two years at Brooke Charter Schools. These were an amazing two years that confirmed that I wanted to dedicate my career to teaching young students, so I went back to school to earn a masters in educational policy from Harvard University Graduate School of Education.
